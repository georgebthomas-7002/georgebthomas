# George B. Thomas - Speaking & Training Summary

*Last Updated: January 9, 2025*

## Overview

George B. Thomas is a professional speaker, trainer, and emcee with over 25 years of sales and marketing experience. He is the creator of the HubHeroes Podcast, owner of Sidekick Strategies, and visionary behind the Superhuman Framework. Known as the "Chief HubSpot Helper," George holds a record-breaking 40+ HubSpot certifications and has spoken at HubSpot's INBOUND conference every year since 2015.

## Speaking Style

George's presentations are described as "transformative experiences packed with bold ideas, real talk, and actionable strategies." His approach:

- **High Energy**: Known for enthusiasm and passion that engages audiences
- **Vulnerable Authenticity**: Opens with personal stories, including his journey as a high school dropout
- **Actionable Content**: Provides tools, strategies, and frameworks audiences can implement
- **Human-Centric**: Focuses on genuine connection over tactics

### Origin Story - The First Keynote
Before his first keynote, Marcus Sheridan advised George to open by sharing that he was a high school dropout. George initially resisted, but Marcus told him: "Until you embrace being vulnerable, you're going to miss out on the magic in life." George took the advice, and afterward a woman told him his story deeply resonated with her. This experience solidified his speaking philosophy of authentic vulnerability.
