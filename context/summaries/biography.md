## The Transformation Story

### Origin Moment
At 17 years old, a math teacher told George in front of the entire class: **"You will never amount to anything."** He believed those words, and about six weeks later, he dropped out of high school and joined the Navy. For years, those words haunted him and dictated his direction in life.

### The Redemption Arc
Today, George has transformed that painful moment into the fuel for his mission. He's no longer angry about what happened - he's grateful. He says he wouldn't take the lost years back if offered, and if he saw that math teacher today, he would thank him. As George puts it: **"He tossed fuel on the fire that created the man - and the empowering educator - I am today."**

### Key Turning Points

1. **2007 - Spiritual Awakening:** While serving as a youth pastor in Ohio and working at Pizza Hut, George expressed frustration about unfulfilled blessings. A prophetess named Juanita told him: **"When are you gonna realize you're the blessing?"** This redirected his focus from seeking blessings to becoming one.

2. **2012 - HubSpot Discovery:** At his first INBOUND conference, something fundamentally changed in his brain. **"The humanity-focused message of HubSpot spoke to my soul and my purpose."**

3. **First Keynote - Vulnerability Breakthrough:** At dinner at the Cheesecake Factory before his first keynote, Marcus Sheridan advised him to open with being a high school dropout. After George resisted, Marcus said: **"Until you embrace being vulnerable, you're going to miss out on the magic in life."** George took the advice, opening with his dropout story, which led to a powerful 30-minute conversation with an attendee about life and forgiveness - and revealed his true calling.

## The Superhuman Framework

George's signature methodology for personal and organizational transformation.

### The Four Cornerstones

1. **Purpose** - The underlying "why" behind everything; what drives you when exhausted
2. **Passion** - The fire; what gets you excited to show up and do the work
3. **Persistence** - The grit that keeps us moving when passion alone isn't enough
4. **Love** - What amplifies the other cornerstones; not just a feeling, but a choice and action

## Family & Faith

### Family Role
George is a dedicated father and husband who prioritizes family above all. His business is built around the **"FEAM"** concept - a blend of "family" and "team" where his wife and children work collaboratively. As his wife notes, they operate as "friends" rather than traditional client relationships.

### Faith Background
George is a man of faith who sees purpose beyond himself. His background as a youth pastor and youth leader at his local church in Ohio deeply influenced his human-centered approach to business and education.

### Church Origins
His creative agency journey actually started at church, where he fell in love with his creative side through building projection systems for songs/sermon notes and creating the church website. It was there he discovered the intersection of creativity and service.
