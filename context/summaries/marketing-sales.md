## Overview

George B. Thomas is a marketing and sales thought leader with **30+ years of experience** and **42+ HubSpot certifications**. He is the owner of **Sidekick Strategies** (a HubSpot Partner Agency), creator of the **HubHeroes Podcast**, host of **Marketing Smarts** at MarketingProfs, and the visionary behind the **Superhuman Framework**.

George has spoken at HubSpot's **INBOUND conference every year since 2015** and has trained countless businesses, agencies, and teams on HubSpot and inbound marketing methodologies.

---

## Core Philosophy

### Human-Centered Marketing

George's approach centers on the belief that **marketing should feel good, build trust, and convert effectively** - all while keeping the process human, genuine, and impactful.

> "Humans don't buy from brands. They buy from other Humans. Without authenticity, empathy, and purpose, you're leaving relationships and revenue on the table."

### Content Creation Philosophy

> "Many times people try to sound smart vs. just sounding helpful. If you come from a content creation place of 'it's all about the humans,' you quickly realize that simplifying the complex, writing to be understood, and leaving the jargon at the door are all superpowers."

### Modern Sales Approach

> "Stop selling like it's 2010. Build trust, forge authentic connections that truly matter, and close deals faster."

## The Superhuman Framework

George created the **Superhuman Framework** - a practical roadmap for personal and professional success built around 10 core principles called "the 10 H's":

### The 10 H Pillars

1. **Holistic** - Viewing the complete picture and ensuring harmony across work, life, and health
2. **Happiness** - Prioritizing joy as central to daily routine
3. **Hungry (Healthy Hustle)** - Pursuing goals with sustainable ambition without burnout
4. **Helpfulness** - Supporting team members, family, and community
5. **Humor** - Finding lightness in challenges
6. **Humility** - Remaining grounded and open to continuous learning
7. **Honesty** - Maintaining authenticity with oneself and others
8. **Health** - Caring for physical, mental, and emotional well-being
9. **Humanity** - Practicing empathy, kindness, and respect
10. **Holiness** - Connecting with purpose and meaning beyond oneself

> "I want to help solve these big-picture problems...help people not just do their jobs better, but live better, feel better, and be better."
