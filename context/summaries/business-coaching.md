## Executive Summary

George B. Thomas is a HubSpot expert, business coach, professional speaker, and entrepreneur who has built a personal brand around being "The HubSpot Helper" and helping humans flourish in business and life. ## The Superhuman Framework

George's signature coaching methodology built around four cornerstones:

### Four Cornerstones

1. **Love** - Deep connection with yourself, others, and your work; the energy that fuels relationships
2. **Purpose** - Not just what you do, but WHY you do it; the driving force behind meaningful action
3. **Passion** - Your emotional engine that makes the grind worth it; when passionate, discipline becomes effortless
4. **Persistence** - Sustained effort paired with health; "healthy hustle" that avoids burnout

### Framework Purpose
> "A dashboard for your life. It gives you the tools to check in on 10 areas regularly, helping you identify where you're thriving and where you might need extra focus."
