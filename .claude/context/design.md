# Design Context

## Direction
Warm & Personal - approachable, trustworthy, human connection

## Color Palette
- **Primary:** #2d4a6f (deep navy)
- **Accent:** #e07a5f (warm coral)
- **Accent Light:** #f4a261 (golden orange)
- **Background:** #faf9f7 (warm white)
- **Text:** #1a1a2e (near black)
- **Text Muted:** #6b7280 (gray)

## Typography
- **Headings:** Playfair Display (serif)
- **Body:** Inter (sans-serif)
- **Scale:** Fluid (clamp-based)

## Animations
- Scroll-triggered via Intersection Observer
- Types: fade-in, slide-up, slide-right, scale-in, stagger
- Durations: fast (200ms), normal (300ms), slow (500ms)
- Easing: ease-out, spring (cubic-bezier)

## Hero Image Treatment
- **Style:** Stacked Frames (user approved)
- Two decorative frames offset behind photo
- Back frame: coral, -6deg rotation
- Middle frame: lighter coral, -3deg rotation
- Hover: frames shift, image lifts slightly
